Urban Forest
Ecosystems Institute

The Urban Forest Ecosystems Institute (UFEI) at Cal Poly addresses the increasing need for improved management of the urban forests in California.

Whether looking for tree selection, identification, or health, urban wood use, urban forest management information, or just looking at pictures, our resources are here to help.
